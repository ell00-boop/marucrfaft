Minecraft Earth

Minecraft Earth was an augmented reality (AR) game developed by Mojang Studios, allowing players to explore, collect resources, and build structures in the real world using AR.

  • Release Date: October 17, 2019
  • Developers: Mojang Studios
  • Platforms: Mobile (iOS, Android)

Minecraft Earth

Minecraft Earth allows players to experience Minecraft in a completely new way using augmented reality. Build, explore, and create in a world layered over your own.

Game Overview

In Minecraft Earth, players collect resources, build creations on "buildplates," and place them in the real world using AR. The game emphasizes collaboration, allowing players to work together on massive builds.

Features

  • Augmented Reality Gameplay: Build and explore in a real-world environment using AR.
  • Collaborative Building: Work with friends to create large-scale constructions.
  • Adventure Mode: Fight mobs and explore dungeons that appear in your physical world.
  • Resource Collection: Gather materials by exploring the real world around you.

Gameplay Mechanics

Players can collect resources, build structures on buildplates, and then place their creations in the real world using AR. They can also embark on adventures, fight mobs, and gather resources.

System Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: iOS 10 or Android 7
  • Processor: ARMv7 processor (Android) / A9 chip (iOS)
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Storage: 300 MB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: iOS 12 or Android 8
  • Processor: A12 chip (iOS) / Snapdragon 845 (Android)
  • Memory: 3 GB RAM
  • Storage: 500 MB available space
