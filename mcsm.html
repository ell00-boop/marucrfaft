Minecraft: Story Mode

Minecraft: Story Mode is an episodic graphic adventure based on the Minecraft video game, developed by Telltale Games. It introduces a new narrative-driven experience within the Minecraft universe.

  • Release Date: October 13, 2015
  • Developers: Telltale Games
  • Platforms: PC, Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch, mobile

Minecraft: Story Mode

Minecraft: Story Mode allows players to experience a new story set in the Minecraft universe, where they can make choices that influence the outcome of the narrative.

Game Overview

In this episodic adventure, players control a character named Jesse and embark on a journey to save the world of Minecraft from various threats. Players must solve puzzles and make decisions that impact the story's direction.

Features

  • Choice-Based Gameplay: Your decisions affect the story and how characters interact with you.
  • Engaging Storyline: Follow an original narrative filled with adventure, mystery, and humor.
  • Co-op Mode: Play with friends and enjoy a shared experience.
  • Exploration: Discover new areas and gather resources to aid in your quest.

Gameplay Mechanics

Players navigate through a combination of quick-time events and dialogue choices, allowing them to experience the narrative in a dynamic way. The game includes exploration elements, puzzles, and combat sequences.

System Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 or later
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo 2.4 GHz or AMD equivalent
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce 8500 / ATI Radeon HD 2600
  • Storage: 2 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7 or later
  • Processor: Intel i5 or equivalent
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 or AMD Radeon HD 6850
  • Storage: 2 GB available space
